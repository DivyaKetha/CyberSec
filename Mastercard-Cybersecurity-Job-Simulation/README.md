# Mastercard Cybersecurity Job Simulation - Phishing Email Design

## Project Overview
Completed Mastercard's Cybersecurity job simulation on Forage, focusing on designing a phishing email simulation campaign. Worked as a Security Awareness Analyst to create realistic phishing scenarios for employee training.

## My Role
- Security Awareness Team Analyst at Mastercard
- Designed phishing simulation campaigns
- Created believable phishing emails to test employee awareness
- Developed training materials based on simulation results

## Investigation Process

### 1. Understanding Phishing Threats
**What I Learned:**
- Phishing is pretending to be someone/something to steal information
- Attackers use links and attachments to infect systems
- Successful phishing costs companies millions
- Employee education is the best defense
