# Phishing Email Analysis: Obvious Fake vs Improved Version

## Original Email Analysis

### Sender: mastercardsIT@gmail.com
**Why It's Suspicious:**
- ❌ Gmail address (not Mastercard domain)
- ❌ No legitimate company would use free email
- ❌ "mastercardsIT" - misspelled domain

### Subject: URGENT! Password Reset Required—
**Why It's Suspicious:**
- ❌ ALL CAPS - screams phishing
- ❌ Multiple exclamation marks
- ❌ Unnecessary dash at end
- ❌ Too generic

### Body Analysis
**Greeting:** "Hello (insert name)"
- ❌ Generic placeholder
- ❌ No personalization

**Content:** "Your email account has been compromised"
- ❌ Too dramatic
- ❌ No specific details
- ❌ Makes people suspicious

**Action Required:** "immediate action is required"
- ❌ Grammar error (immediate should be capitalized)
- ❌ Creates panic, not rational response

**Link:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phishing
- ❌ Wikipedia link is obvious
- ❌ No masking/hiding
- ❌ "Click here" is suspicious

**Closing:** "Regards, Mastercard IT"
- ❌ No contact information
- ❌ Too vague

---

## Improved Email Analysis

### Sender: it.support@mastercard.com
**Why It's Believable:**
- ✅ Correct Mastercard domain
- ✅ "IT Support" sounds legitimate
- ✅ Professional email format

### Subject: Password Reset Required For Corporate Account
**Why It's Believable:**
- ✅ Proper capitalization
- ✅ Specific (Corporate Account)
- ✅ Professional tone

### Body Analysis

**Greeting:** "Hello Josh,"
- ✅ Personalization
- ✅ Professional greeting

**Context:** "As part of the company security risk assessment..."
- ✅ Provides legitimate-sounding reason
- ✅ Sounds official
- ✅ Use security terminology

**Action Required:** "It is mandatory to take immediate action..."
- ✅ Clear instructions
- ✅ Professional tone
- ✅ Reasonable deadline (60 minutes)

**Link:** "Click here to reset your password" (Masked)
- ✅ Link hidden behind text
- ✅ Looks trustworthy
- ✅ Clear call to action

**Additional Info:** "Ignore if already done..."
- ✅ Lowers suspicion
- ✅ Appears helpful
- ✅ Reduces panic

**Contact:** "Contact IT Support Team"
- ✅ Offers help
- ✅ Sounds legitimate
- ✅ Professional

**Closing:** "Mastercard IT Support"
- ✅ Full organization name
- ✅ Professional sign-off
